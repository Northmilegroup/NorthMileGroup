What We Do

Analytical rigor.
Operational results.

We work with transportation teams that have outgrown spreadsheets and need structured, defensible analysis — not just a report, but a roadmap.

Freight Analytics
Specialized in LTL — lane-level cost benchmarking, carrier performance scoring, and spend analysis that surfaces the gaps your TMS isn't showing you. We speak the language of LTL pricing natively: discounts, minimums, and accessorial exposure included.
Transportation Optimization
Mode selection, load planning, and routing analysis designed to cut cost per unit moved — without sacrificing service levels.
NMFC Classification & Class Auditing
Incorrect freight classification is one of the most common — and costly — billing errors in LTL. We audit your commodity descriptions, assign correct NMFC codes and freight classes, and identify reclassification opportunities that reduce invoice disputes and prevent carrier overcharges.
Network Design
DC placement, carrier mix, and flow path modeling to build a distribution network that scales with your volume — not against it.
